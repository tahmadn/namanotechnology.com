About Us

At Namano, we turn messy, scattered data into something clean, organized, and useful. Whether you're juggling spreadsheets, outdated databases, or even stacks of paper files, we bring clarity, structure, and security to your stored information — so you can make smarter decisions without the data headaches.

We're a small team with deep roots. With decades of experience in data conversion, cleaning, and restructuring, we know how messy data can throw off a business. Our job is to fix it—and we take that job seriously.

A Note from Our Founder

"I started Namano Technology Inc. back in 2001, but my journey with data started long before that. I've spent more than 30 years in IT, mainly in banking and software services, and I've always had a thing for finding patterns and making messy stuff make sense.

Early in my career, I was put in charge of large-scale data migration projects for financial institutions—everything from ancient ledger cards to outdated digital systems got transformed into secure, modern platforms. These weren't simple tasks. They demanded intense attention to detail, validation, and restructuring of massive datasets to meet high-stakes compliance and security requirements.

Eventually, I became Head of Information Systems at a major bank, where I built the IT department from the ground up and oversaw the organization's full transition to digital. Later, I launched my own consultancy and worked with banks and credit unions across the Caribbean for many years.

These days, I volunteer with a national advocacy group, cleaning and compiling data into structured, searchable formats. Whether it's for banking systems or nonprofits, every project I take on keeps me grounded in what I love: making data useful again.

Security, privacy and reliability have always been non-negotiable for me. I apply that same discipline to every project we take on here at Namano."
